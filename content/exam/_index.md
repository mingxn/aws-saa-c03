---
title: "Exam Mode"
---

Take a timed exam simulation to test your readiness for the AWS Solutions Architect Associate (SAA-C03) certification exam.

## Exam Mode Features

- **Timed Experience**: Countdown timer simulates real exam pressure (default 130 minutes)
- **No Immediate Feedback**: Answers are not revealed until you submit the exam
- **Free Navigation**: Move between questions freely, but no answers shown during the exam
- **Flag for Review**: Mark questions to revisit before submitting
- **Submit All at Once**: Review your performance after completing all questions
- **Performance Breakdown**: See your score by exam domain
- **Full Review**: Review all questions with explanations after submission

Configure your exam session below and click "Start Exam" when ready.
